Cracking Wi-Fi: Breaching Network Security

Published on: [Date]

In this thrilling demonstration, I show how a hacker could break into a poorly secured Wi-Fi network by cracking weak encryption protocols like WEP. Using my university dormitory network, I explore the dangers of Wi-Fi vulnerabilities.

Vulnerabilities:

Outdated security protocols like WEP leave networks exposed to brute-force attacks, allowing hackers to intercept data.

Protection Tips:

Using WPA3 encryption and monitoring tools is critical to securing your network. In this video, I provide real-world examples of security breaches due to compromised Wi-Fi networks.
